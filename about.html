Hello! I'm Aidan.

I'm a machine learning researcher working towards neural networks that can reason like humans. I'm interested in models of attention and memory, dynamic and modular neural networks, discrete search problems, and systematic generalization. I'm also curious about exploration and multi-agent communication in reinforcement learning.

Right now, I'm a research scientist at NVIDIA working on automated theorem proving with reinforcement learning. In the past, I've worked with Descartes Labs to understand satellite imagery and find trees with computer vision, as well as Anima Anandkumar's lab at Caltech to study why neural networks have such a hard time learning arithmetic. I recently graduated from Caltech, where I studied computer science and taught a class on deep learning.
