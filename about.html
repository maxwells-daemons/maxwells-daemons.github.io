Hello! I'm Aidan.

I'm a machine learning researcher interested in models of attention and memory, as well as neural networks that learn to reason in a structured way. I'm also curious about exploration and multi-agent cooperation in reinforcement learning.

Right now, I'm working in Anima Anandkumar's lab at Caltech on improving the abilities of neural networks to systematically generalize. In the past, I've worked with Descartes Labs to understand satellite imagery with computer vision. I was recently an undergrad at Caltech.
