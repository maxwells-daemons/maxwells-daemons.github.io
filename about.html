Hello! I'm Aidan.

I'm a machine learning researcher interested in models of attention and memory, as well as modular neural networks that learn to reason in a structured way. I'm also curious about exploration and multi-agent communication in reinforcement learning.

Right now, I'm working in Anima Anandkumar's lab at Caltech on improving the abilities of neural networks to systematically generalize. In the past, I've worked with Descartes Labs to understand satellite imagery and find trees with computer vision. I recently graduated from Caltech, where I studied computer science.
